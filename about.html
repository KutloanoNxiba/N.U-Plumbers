About PrimeFix Solutions

Learn more about our company, our values, and our team

Our Story

Founded in 2015, PrimeFix Solutions began as a small family-owned business with a passion for quality craftsmanship and exceptional customer service. What started as a local plumbing service quickly expanded to include electrical work as we recognized the need for reliable, multi-skilled home service professionals in our community.

Today, we're proud to serve residential and commercial clients across the region, maintaining our commitment to integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction in every job we undertake.

Our Mission

At PrimeFix Solutions, our mission is to provide top-quality plumbing and electrical services with unparalleled professionalism and reliability. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients by delivering exceptional service, honest advice, and fair pricing.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We do what's right, even when no one is watching.
  • Quality: We stand behind our work with a satisfaction guarantee.
  • Reliability: We show up on time and complete the job as promised.
  • Expertise: Our team undergoes continuous training to stay current with industry standards.
  • Community: We're proud to serve our local community with pride and dedication.
